# Policy 002: Decision-Making

**Date:** 2026-04-07
**Status:** Active

## Purpose
Clarify how decisions are made at different levels of the organization.

## Intended Outcome
Fast operational decisions, informed tactical decisions, and well-considered strategic decisions.

## Policy Details

| Level | Scope | Who Decides | Process |
|-------|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Operational** | Day-to-day work within a domain | Agent (role keeper) | Act autonomously within role boundaries |
| **Tactical** | Priorities, backlog ordering, dispatch | Alex | Agent recommends, Alex decides |
| **Strategic** | Product direction, phase transitions, pivots | Alex | Research-informed, consent-based with community input (when community exists) |

### Consent Process (for strategic decisions)
1. Present the proposal with driver and requirement
2. Gather input from affected agents/domains
3. Check for objections (evidence of harm or missed improvement)
4. Resolve objections or amend proposal
5. Decide and record in the logbook

## Responsibilities

| Who | What |
|-----|------|
| Agents | Make operational decisions, recommend tactical ones |
| Coordinator | Surface decisions that need Alex's attention |
| Alex | Make tactical and strategic decisions |

## Metrics and Monitoring

| Metric | How Monitored | Frequency |
|--------|--------------|-----------|
| Decision speed | Time from proposal to decision | Quarterly |
| Decision quality | Outcomes vs. intended outcomes | Quarterly |
