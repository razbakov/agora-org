# Ikigai Team — Organization Canvas

## Purpose
Enable individuals to build a personal AI governance team that represents their values in collective decision-making through liquid democracy and trust circles.

## Overall Strategy
See `00_Organization_Logbook/03_Strategy.md`

## Customers, Partners, and Stakeholders
- **Primary users:** Individuals who want an AI team for personal/professional governance (early adopters: developers, knowledge workers, civic tech enthusiasts)
- **Secondary users:** Communities experimenting with liquid democracy and decentralized governance
- **Partners:** Democracy Earth Foundation (dormant but foundational research), open-source governance projects, S3/sociocracy practitioners
- **Stakeholders:** Anyone affected by decisions made through the platform (broader public as adoption grows)

## Business Model(s)
- **Open-source framework** — the core Ikigai Team setup (agents, coaching, OKRs) is free and open-source
- **Premium features** — hosted agent infrastructure, advanced delegation protocols, team/organization tiers
- **Consulting/training** — helping organizations adopt the governance framework
- Revenue model to be validated through experiments

## Environmental Conditions
- **Legal/Economic:** AI governance is unregulated but evolving rapidly; GDPR applies to personal data processing; no legal framework for AI delegation in political systems yet
- **Market trends:** Explosion of AI agent frameworks (AutoGPT, CrewAI, etc.); growing distrust in traditional democratic institutions; rising interest in liquid democracy and DAO governance
- **Competition:** Democracy Earth (dormant), Loomio (active, consensus-focused), Aragon (DAO tooling), generic AI agent frameworks (lack governance/values alignment). No competitor combines personal coaching + AI agents + liquid democracy.
